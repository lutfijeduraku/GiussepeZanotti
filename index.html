Product Details

Jewels of the Sea - Giuseppe Zanotti Icons returns with 'Coralli' MILAN - The sea and its colors on women's feet. The Giuseppe Zanotti Icons project presents its third exclusive chapter, entitled Coralli - this exclusive drop is a summer story, which features two models of handcrafted sandals. Inspired by two archival icons - the Spipiott flat sandal (2004) and the Coralli platform sandal (2005) - Coralli are sophisticated and fun jewel sandals, timeless yet extremely contemporary. In other words, perfect for the return of summer and glamor. The flat model is a thong sandal, in golden leather, adorned with a fish-shaped jewel, covered with colored crystals. While the sandal with stiletto heel, in silver leather, is characterized by a coral, also enriched with crystals.
